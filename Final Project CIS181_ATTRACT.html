Chitzen Itza

Chichén Itzá is one of the most awe-inspiring ancient cities in the world. It offers a vast amount of Mayan architecture hidden in the jungles of Mexico’s Yucatán Peninsula. At the heart of Itza El Castillo. El Castillo is a pyramid built with such precision that it creates a shadowy serpent during the spring and fall equinoxes. This is truly fascinating as the Mayan people of this area worshiped the snake God Kukulkan. Many sculture surounding the are inspired by the snake God. White in Itza you will be able to walk among sacred temples, explore intricate carvings, and stand in the same spot where ancient astronomers once mapped the stars. Chichén Itzá offers an experience that feels like stepping into another world.

Mayan ruins of Tulum

The Tulum ruins are a breathtaking blend of history and scenery — an ancient Mayan city perched on a cliff overlooking the deep blue Caribbean Sea. Once a bustling port and fortress, its stone temples and ocean views offer a powerful glimpse into the past. With its unique coastal setting and rich cultural legacy, Tulum is the perfect place to explore ancient wonders and enjoy paradise at the same time.
